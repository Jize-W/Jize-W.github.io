Jize Wang

PhD Student, Department of Automation, SJTU

I am a first-year PhD student at Department of Automation at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), under the supervision of Prof. Cailian Chen and Prof. Xinyi Le. I am also a research intern at Shanghai AI Laboratory, mentored by Dr. Yining Li and Dr. Kai Chen. Prior to that, I received the B.Eng. degree from SJTU in 2022.

My current research interest mainly lies in LLM-based agents, multi-agent systems, and information extraction. For further discussion or potential collaboration, please email me at: jizewang2000 [at] gmail [dot] com.
